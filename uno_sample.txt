//= RULES =//
Each player starts out with 7 cards. There will be a card pile to draw from and another pile
to place cards. When placing cards into the pile:
    * If drawing based on color, the color MUST match with the card on top of the stack.
      Number or action can be different.
    * If drawing based on number, the number MUST match with the card on top of the stack. 
      Color can be different.
    * Number and color can match. Nothing special happens.
    * Players can place multiple cards of the same number. (i.e. Red 7 and Yellow 7 is valid, but
      Blue 9 and Blue Skip is not)
    * Players can place multiple cards of the same action, EXCEPT Wild card. (i.e 2 +4's or 3 Reverse's
      are valid, but a Reverse and a Skip are not)
    * Players can place multiple cards of the same number and color. (i.e 2 green 6's and 1 red 6 is valid)
    * When you need to take a card from the draw pile, you can choose to place it into the stack, but it
      must be valid.
    * Whoever is the first to 0 cards is the winner, and the game ends!
